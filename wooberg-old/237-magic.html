237 Magic

For this variant you may only use cards that have a casting cost of 2, 3 or 7 and land. You may play cards that have additional cost, like kicker and buyback, as long as the base cost is 2, 3, or 7. For split cards do not count the casting cost of both side combined. Each side must meet the requirements. For example you could play Fire/Ice because both side have a casting cost of 2.

Banned and Restricted List

No individual cards are banned in this format.  Usually players agree on a Wizards of the Coast sanctioned format and play according to that restricted/banned list.
